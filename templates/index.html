Personal projects.
QRShare
While traveling with a friend who loves taking street photos in a foreign place, we chatted about how tricky it could be to share those pictures with the people she snaps, especially if we don't speak their language. So, we discussed about how to do it. We came up with the idea of using QR codes to share pictures easily. Here's the deal: the photographer takes pics, upload them on a server, and gets a QR code. The model scans the QR code, and toudoum toudoum *washing machine drum roll* ! You're in a folder with the pics. From there, you can do stuff like download the pics, send them to an email, or even delete them if you want. Simple, right?
